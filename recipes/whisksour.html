whiskey sour (the real way)

ingredients

  • whiskey, preferred brand (2 oz) ( high quality rye recommended )
  • lemon juice (1 oz)
  • simple syrup (1/2 oz)
  • egg white (from one egg lol, optional but adds some je ne se quoi)

* consuming raw eggs may pose a food borne illness, proceed at your own risk *

instructions

  1. add all ingredients into a shaker
  2. shake once, dry
  3. add ice, shake again
  4. pour into glass of choice and enjoy
