asparagus (the cheesy way)

ingredients

  • asparagus
  • salt n pepper
  • mozzarella
  • parmesan
  • olive oil

instructions

  1. cut the asparagus and line on a baking sheet
  2. drizzle oil on the asparagus, season to taste
  3. throw a lot of cheese all over, and bake for 20 minutes at 375
