wagyu ribeye (the simple way)

ingredients

  • an a5 8oz wagyu ribeye steak
  • butter
  • garlic
  • thyme
  • salt & pepper (to taste)

* consuming raw meat may pose a food borne illness, proceed at your own risk *

instructions

  1. heat up your pan to be ripping hot (and preferably a cast iron)
  2. add about half a stick of butter and let it melt
  3. once fully melted but not burnt, add your steak and lower the heat. cook to desired temperature, i usually do 4-5 minutes per side.
  4. add more butter, the garlic, and lower the temperature. baste the steak for the remaining time and desired doneness.
  5. pour the remaining butter over steak, let rest for minimum 5 minutes, and then serve!
